How CashRaaga works

Built for busy people – no budgets to maintain, no categories to set up. Just your bank statement.

Step 1 · Upload

Download a CSV or Excel statement from your bank or UPI app. Drag & drop into CashRaaga. No login required.

Step 2 · Analyse

Our models detect salary, EMIs, UPI flows and recurring patterns in seconds. Everything runs in memory on the server.

Step 3 · See ahead

View where money is going, your EMI stress, and what your next month is likely to look like – before you actually spend it.
