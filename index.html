How it works
No signup, no onboarding funnel — just a quick three-step flow.
Step 1
Export your statement
Download a CSV or Excel statement from your net-banking app for 3–6 months.
Step 2
Upload & map columns
Tell CashRaaga which columns are Date, Description and Amount; CR/DR is optional.
Step 3
Review your snapshot
See UPI flows, EMIs, categories, savings trend and a simple forecast — and download a cleaned CSV.
