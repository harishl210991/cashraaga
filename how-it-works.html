How CashRaaga Works

CashRaaga helps you see your cashflow before it happens. Upload your bank statement (CSV or Excel), and the app instantly gives you:

  • UPI inflow & outflow summary
  • EMI load overview
  • Monthly savings snapshot
  • Spending categories
  • AI-based next-month savings prediction

CashRaaga accepts CSV or Excel files from all major banks.
