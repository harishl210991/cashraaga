How CashRaaga works
Simple upload → mapped columns → instant cashflow snapshot.
Step 1
Export your statement
Download a CSV or Excel bank statement from your net-banking app (monthly or multi-month).
Step 2
Upload & map columns
Upload the file into CashRaaga and tell it which columns are Date, Description and Amount. If your bank uses CR/DR, you can map that too.
Step 3
Automatic classification
CashRaaga parses each row, separates inflows/outflows, tags obvious categories (UPI, EMIs, rent, subscriptions, food, shopping, fuel, utilities) and groups them month-wise.
Step 4
Insights & forecast
You see monthly savings, UPI net outflow, EMI load and a simple forecast of likely savings for the next few months. You can also download a cleaned CSV for your records.
In short: Statement in → UPI, EMI & savings out → next-month signal.
