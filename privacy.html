Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

CashRaaga (“we”, “our”, “us”) is a personal finance insights tool designed to analyze your bank statement locally and provide clear summaries of spending, UPI flows, EMIs, and savings forecasts.

1. No data is uploaded or stored

CashRaaga processes your bank statement directly inside your browser or local machine. Your files are never uploaded to any server, including our own.

2. What data we do not collect

  • No bank statements
  • No UPI IDs
  • No EMIs or transactions
  • No login or authentication
  • No personal identity details

3. Anonymous analytics (optional)

We may collect anonymous traffic data (page views, browser type, region) to improve the app. This data contains:

  • No transaction information
  • No financial information
  • No identifiers

4. Third-party services

CashRaaga uses trusted hosting providers like GitHub Pages and Streamlit Cloud. These services do not access your statement data.

5. Cookies

We may use minimal technical cookies for performance, not for tracking.

6. Your consent

By using CashRaaga, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
